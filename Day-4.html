DAY-4

This time I woke up at 5 AM. Everyone was getting ready. I was lucky to catch up, and I got ready in 5 minutes. We went to see the Rameshwaram temple. When we got there, there was no crowd, and we went inside, but the policeman stopped us because phones and shorts were not allowed. So we kept the phones in a locker for shorts. Sara uncle had an idea. He bought a dhoti from a nearby shop and told the others to wear dhoti. We went inside the temple, but the temple was without a rush. We finished the darshan in 10 minutes. But it took us half an hour to complete the Amman darshan. We ate breakfast in Guru mess at 8:00 AM. Before we went to Dhanushkodi we went to a small temple. It looked like the Mahabalipuram temple.
